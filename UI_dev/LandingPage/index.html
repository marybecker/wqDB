about the portal

The portal provides links needed to upload, retrieve and explore ambient water data collected by the CT DEEP Water Planning Division. This includes ambient biological, physical, chemical, temperature/conductivity logger (eventually) and stream connectivity (eventually) data. Data is collected in rivers, streams, lakes and estuaries across Connecticut.

Water Quality Portal

Final QAed ambient water quality data collected by CT can be found here. The Water Quality Portal (WQP) is a cooperative service sponsored by the United States Geological Survey (USGS), the Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), and the National Water Quality Monitoring Council (NWQMC). It serves data collected by over 400 state, federal, tribal, and local agencies.

SHEDS Stream Temperature Database

The SHEDS Stream Temperature Database website is a portal for viewing, downloading, uploading, and managing stream temperature data across the northeastern U.S. The Stream Temperature database is part of the Spatial Hydro-Ecological Decision System (SHEDS).
