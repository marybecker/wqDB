about the portal

The portal provides links needed to upload, retrieve and explore ambient water data collected by the CT DEEP Water Planning Division. This includes ambient biological, physical, chemical, temperature/conductivity logger (eventually) and stream connectivity (eventually) data. Data is collected in rivers, streams, lakes and estuaries across Connecticut.
